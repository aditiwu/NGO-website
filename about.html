SIDDHAM is a group of Supportive Intellectual Dare Devil's Having Assertive Mindset working for the welfare of the society. It aims at creating awareness among the youth of the society through it's various programs thus helping them in becoming a responsible citizens. With it's inception in the early Jan 2018 SIDDHAM has a mission of empowering women to make them independent and more confident. With the focus on the youth of the society it has been able to conduct various programs on cancer awareness, health camps, menstrual hygiene, etc.

With a vision of reaching out to the maximum number of people through it's various programs SIDDHAM has signed up an MOU with Govt. degree college for women begumpet with a strength of almost 3000 girls mostly rural. Also it has plans to adopt the abhilasha vocational centre for the intellectually challenged people.







Our Vision




sometext


Siddham has a long-term vision to collaborate with various government and private sector departments thereby creating and fostering entrepreneurial opportunities. To create a homogenous society for the women to pursue their skills, becoming entrepreneurs, and encouraging sustainable business models for small time women entrepreneurs encouraging them to utilize their skill which can help them make them financially independent.

We mostly focus on empowerment of women in rural areas as they need support in making them understand and educate and scale up their capabilities creating suitable platforms. We intend to conduct exhibitions by providing space at minimal cost as this can help people get in touch with their end customers which is more profitable and satisfactory and also makes them understand the customers need.
